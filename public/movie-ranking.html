The Best Movies According to Jose

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Pianist

Really captivating tragic story set during WWII.

Top Gun Maverick

Cool and engaging action movie about pilots and jets and using practical effects that stay true to its conventional action cinematography.

Spiderverse: Across the Spiderverse

Super beautiful film. It has a unique stylized animation that really represents the various artists involved in making the film.
